Music

Listen to your music at high quality
- Listen to your favourite music with your friends at high quality audio!
Have full control over what is being played
- Be able to control what goes on in your playlist, with the ability to remove songs, clear the queue, shuffle the queue, monitor volume and more

- You are also able to add playlists and individual songs from YouTube and Spotify with powerful searching algorithms to give you the most accurate results.

- You can also search for the lyrics to a particular song and search a song title by its lyrics!

Logging

user infractions
- Log user infractions such as warns, mutes, kicks, bans etc that can be retrieved at any time in a paginated and scrollable embed!
User levelling
- Turn on and off user levelling systems and guild leaderboard to compete for the most XP, reach the highest levels and fight to be on top of the leaderboard!

TTS

Discord chat TTS
- Listen to and what's being said in the text channel with Text To Speech in your voice channel!
Manage whos messages are read out!
It is easy to manage whos messages are read out to the voice channel; you are able to add and remove users and give certain users permission to also manage whose messages are being read out!

Hypixel

Gather Hypixel information
- Get the statistics on a given user from Hypixel, such as their current game, when they were last active and their bedwars stats!
Compare your stats
Compare your Hypixel statistics with your Hypixel friends with a simple command!
Welcome messages

Greet your new members with a warm welcome as they join the server, and a farewell as they leave!

Levels and XP

Allow users to gain XP and advance in levels as they chat and become more active within the guild, and display the top 10 most active members within your community on a leaderboard!

Also optionally select a channel where announcements for users levelling up are sent. This can be changed at any time!

Track your members

Track when members leave and join your guild with a membercount channel which shows an up-to-date real time count!

Customisable behaviour

Have you ever been using multiple bots and you get prefix conflictions? Well fret not, because this bot allows you to change the prefix for your guild with one simple command!

Enjoy Minecraft from Discord

You can get statistics and information from both Minecraft and Hypixel!

  • Configurable channels for levels/welcomes

  • Customisable behaviour
  • And much more!

Which all in all, make for a perfect utility selection to manage your server with ease!
